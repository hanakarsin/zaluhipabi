Aged man shares secret to long lasting relationship

February 2024 · 3 minute read

- A video clip of an aged couple speaking about their marriage has gone viral

- The husband identified as Mace Von shared tips on how his marriage of seventy years has lasted the test of time

- The happily married man stated that life is not complex and people should learn to live with kindness and a clear spirit

Some say marriage is not easy and with the rate of divorces happening in this present age, it is easy to believe that maybe it is better to live life alone. However, every now and then, a couple shows up and dispels this belief. With their story, they present hope for those who still believe in the power of love and the beauty of commitment.

A video clip of an aged couple recently surfaced online and it has sparked the right cord in the heart of hopeless romantics. The husband, Mace Von and his pretty wife ran into a woman while shopping and they shared tips on how they have been able to stay in love for seventy years since they first started dating.

Von revealed that they dated for 10 years before walking down the aisle and have been married for 60 years. He also shared that in all their 70 years as a couple, they have never had a fight or a misunderstanding.

Mace stated that being kind and having a clear spirit has helped them in having a successful marriage. The happily married man who still refers to his wife as his bride shared that life is not difficult, most people only try to make it complex.

On how they have made their marriage work, he said: "I always made her feel loved & adored. Every woman should be treated that way. It's simple. Life is really not as hard as we make it and everybody should live kind with a clean heart and the world would be a much better place. That's how we live our lives."

It can be said that the power couple have found the secret of life and it is evident in the way they have remained faithful and loyal to each other.
