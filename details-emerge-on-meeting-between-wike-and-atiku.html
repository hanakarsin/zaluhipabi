Details Emerge On Meeting Between Wike And Atiku

May 2024 · 1 minute read

The Rivers State Governor, Nyesom Wike and the presidential candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), Atiku Abubakar on Thursday met in London.

The meeting between Atiku and Governor is part of the efforts to resolve that crisis rocking the party ahead of the 2023 general elections.

A source that spoke with Punch during the meeting revealed that the former Vice President pleaded with Wike to support him in 2023.

The close associate of Governor Wike further stated that Atiku during the meeting agreed to meet all the demands of Wike.

According to the source, the camp of the Rivers State Governor has agreed to work with Atiku in 2023.

The source noted that the details of the agreement between the PDP presidential candidate and Governor Wike in London will be released.

The source stated, “He (Atiku) has agreed to meet all demands. He will sign an undertaking on all the agreements. Once he can meet them, the Wike group is ready to work with him. The details will be made known.”

The source revealed that the Governor of Adamawa State Governor, Ahmadu Fintri, accompanied the former Vice-President to the meeting.

Atiku’s meeting with Wike is coming moments after the former president, Olusegun Obasanjo met with him in London.
