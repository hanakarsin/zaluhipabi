Wolftyla - Age, Family, Bio

April 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Manhattan-based singer-songwriter who first went viral on Vine in 2014, with her song "Feels" on SoundCloud. Her debut EP, Wolf in Color released in August 2020 featured breakout hits such as "All Tinted", "Butterflies" and "Candy". "All Tinted" has amassed over 9 million views on YouTube and more than 11 million independent streams on Spotify to date. 

Before Fame

She played point guard during her high school days until she later enrolled at NYU Steinhardt to study Music Business. 

Trivia

She launched her own independent brand called "Wolf Members Only". Some of the official music videos she's directed and/or co-directed include All Tinted, Candy, Butterflies, and In the Middle.

Family Life

She is half Korean and half African-American. Her mother's side is from Seoul, Korea, and was raised in Queens. She has two younger sisters and once posted a photo of her mother on her Instagram page thanking her for all her sacrifices. She also owns a Siberian Husky named Cartier King.

Associated With

She co-produced alongside Timbaland on "Candy" a part of her debut EP. She has also worked on hit titles including Megan Thee Stallion's "Simon Says" and Ella Mai's "How" featuring Roddy Ricch. She has also collaborated with League of Legends' virtual pop group, K/DA, for collaborating on "The Baddest" and "DRUM GO DUM". Her early features include Roy Woods and Jay Park.
