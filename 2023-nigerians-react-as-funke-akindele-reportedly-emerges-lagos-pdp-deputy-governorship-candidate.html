Nigerians React As Funke Akindele Reportedly Emerges Lagos PDP Deputy Governorship Candidate

March 2024 · 2 minute read

Nollywood actress and producer, Funke Akindele is topping trends on Twitter after reportedly emerging as the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) deputy gubernatorial candidate for Lagos State.

Naija News earlier reported that Funke secured the position after beating the other five nominees, former governorship aspirant, Gbadebo Rhodes-Vivour; former Senatorial candidate for Lagos East, Yeye Shobajo, another governorship aspirant, David Kolawole Vaughan known as DAKOVA and Engr. Teslim Balogun.

A video of Funke Akindele and the governorship candidate Abdul Azeez Olajide Adediran popularly known as Jandor has gone viral online.

Many Nigerians have expressed mixed reactions over the new position of Funke Akindele, considering it is coming in the wake of her marriage crash.
