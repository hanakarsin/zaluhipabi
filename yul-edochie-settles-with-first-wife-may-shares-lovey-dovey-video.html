'Online Inlaws' In The Mud As Yul Edochie Settles With First Wife May, Shares Lovey-Dovey Video

March 2024 · 1 minute read

Nollywood actor and presidential aspirant Yul Edochie seemed to have settled with his first wife May Edochie over his marriage to his side chick and colleague Judy Austin.

A few weeks ago, Yul Edochie set the internet into a frenzy after announcing the birth of his new baby Star Munachisom and his marriage to Judy Austin.

His first wife May Edochie expressed surprise and anger over the news as she rained curses on her husband and Judy Austin saying God will judge them.

Many Nigerians expressed concern for her as they mercilessly dragged Yul Edochie for betraying his wife considering she got to know about the marriage after Judy’s dowry was paid.

However, Yul Edochie has shut down critics and online inlaws as he took to his Instagram page a few minutes ago to share a lovey-dovey video of himself and his first wife May.

The couple looked so excited inside the car as they sing and danced together.

The video generated mixed reactions online as some netizens claimed it could be an old video.
