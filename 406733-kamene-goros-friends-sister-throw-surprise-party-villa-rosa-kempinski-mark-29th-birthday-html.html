Kamene Goro's Friends, Sister Throw Her Surprise Party at Villa Rosa Kempinski to Mark 29th Birthday

March 2024 · 3 minute read

- Kamene Goro thought she was going on a date with a female friend at Villa Rosa Kempinski on her birthday eve

- The beauty got to one of the rooms only to realise that a birthday party was awaiting her with close friends and her sister present

- They sat together at one table and enjoyed a sumptuous meal and also popped some champagne to celebrate the radio host

Radio presenter Kamene Goro got the surprise of a lifetime during her 29th birthday eve on Tuesday, March 23.

The sassy journalist did not know that a call from a friend to go hangout would turn out to be her birthday party at one of Kenya's most expensive hotels.

Sharing a series of videos on her Instagram page, Kamene could be seen arriving at Villa Rosa Kempinski for a date with a female friend, before she heard people shout "surprise" and realised it was actually her birthday party.

The radio girl recognised that her close friends and sister, who had earlier communicated about their busy schedules, were actually planning a surprise party.

Coincidentally nearly all the ladies, plus Kamene, were dressed in shiny white and gold dresses as they sang and made a toast while gushing over how much they loved her.

Kamene could be seen tearing up after her sister, Natasha Goro, gave a touching speech during a toast as they embraced each other, showing how close they are.

The revelers sat together at one table and enjoyed a sumptuous meal which they definitely enjoyed, going by how happy and satisfied they appeared.

Kamene is ready

In other news, the Kiss 100 FM radio presenter revealed a few weeks back that she was finally ready for a serious relationship.

Kamene said she was not only ready to settle in a romantic affair but also prepared to pamper and treat her significant other as a king.

The beauty said this on Monday, March 1, insisting she would cook, do laundry and iron her man's clothes and all other nice things that a woman should do to her lover.

She also said she had missed having a partner who could ask her about her day and make her feel special.
