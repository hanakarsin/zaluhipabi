Sharon Mundia wedding story

Sharon Mundia the fashion blogger become Mrs. Leteipan officially after she got married to her longtime love and also her fiancé Lonina Leteipan in Watamu on 11th of November 2016 at about 3 pm. Sharon Mundia wedding was themed in all white and was held at one of the best hotels of Watamu named Medina Palms Hotel. The hotel has its own scenic beauty of spotless white beaches and never-ending blue sky which gave the wedding a lavish feel which can mesmerize anyone easily. The wedding ceremony of Sharon witnessed some of the celebrities like Edith Kimani(TV personality), Patricia Kihoro, Miss Mandi, Janet Mbugua( Citizen TV) and Joy Kendi among the other guest who were invited. Everybody was eager to know why all the bridesmaids wore a white dress at that very day. As traditionally in weddings no one wears white except the bride. Sharon Mundia looked stunning in her wedding gown when she came with her father Mundia Muchiri, Media King.
