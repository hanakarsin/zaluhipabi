Mike Sonko Offers to Accommodate, Educate Caroline Kangogo's Children

  • Corporal Caroline Kangogo was found dead in a bathroom in her parents' home in Annin, Elgeyo Marakwet county, on the morning of Friday, July 16
  • According to Rift Valley regional commissioner George Natembeya, the suspected killer cop turned the gun on herself
  • Former Nairobi governor Mike Sonko while mourning questioned the circumstances surrounding her death

Former Nairobi governor Mike Sonko has shown his generous side again after offering to accommodate and educate the late cop Caroline Kangogo's children.

In a statement on his Facebook page on Friday, July 16, the kind-hearted politician said he would gladly take care of the deceased's children if her family becomes overwhelmed.

"My special request to her innocent family in Elgeyo Marakwet, if you experience any difficulties in raising Kangogo's children or educating them, please give them to me; I will warmly host them and educate them. Children are a blessing from God, Sonko said.

The flamboyant politician joined other Kenyans in questioning the circumstances surrounding the corporal's death.

"May God help me to remain silent, but whether she committed suicide or was killed, depression is real, and the same predicament can befall anyone, he said.

Kangogo was found dead in a bathroom in her parents' home in Annin, Elgeyo Marakwet county, on the morning of Friday, July 16.

According to Rift Valley regional commissioner George Natembeya, Kangogo turned the gun to herself, leading to her death.

"She came home at 5am today and stayed around for a while before going to the bathroom where she shot herself dead at around 7am," Natembeya said.

According to Natembeya, her parents were not aware she was at home.

Marriage

Kangogo was married to a senior police officer attached to the Maritime Police Unit in Mombasa and together were blessed with two kids aged eight and 11 years.

Her marriage, however, was troubled as she allegedly fought with him in public over infidelity.

The duo later separated but agreed to co-parent. Before the killing scandal, Kagongo lived with their two children, but the husband has since taken them.

Main suspect behind John Ogweno, Peter Ndwiga's killings

Kangogo was the main suspect behind the killing of Police Constable John Ogweno in Nakuru and Juja businessman Peter Njiru Ndwiga.

For more than a week, the policewoman, described as a sharpshooter, was still on the loose after managing to escape several police dragnets.
