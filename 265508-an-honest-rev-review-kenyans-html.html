Are you in need of a flexible online job that requires little or no experience and is not making sales calls? Well, this work exists; it involves becoming an online transcriptionist. Put simply; transcription consists of the art of listening to audio files and typing out what you hear. With this kind of work, you have the freedom to determine your working hours and can work as much or as little as possible each day. There are several trustworthy transcription organizations available online. One of such companies includes rev.com. The following article will provide insights on how you can kick-start your transcriptionist career at rev.com freelance website as well as tips and tricks for pro-transcribers.

Initially, Rev.com was known as FoxTranscribe and FoxTranslate. The transcription firm was started by two co-workers of oDesk.com, CEO Jason Chicola and Josh Breinleinger in 2010. The company is situated in San Francisco; CA. two years after its founding, Venlky Ganesan of Globespan Capital Partners boosted the company’s expansion by funding it.

Rev.com Application Procedure

Rev.com is among the top transcription firms that accept applicants from all over the globe. What you need is a computer/laptop, a good pair of headsets and a stable internet connection. Please note that rev.com has a strict application procedure and in most cases, just 10% of all applicants get accepted. Even pro-transcribers who have several years of experience still find Rev Application process challenging. Nevertheless, do not be discouraged as it might be different for you.

In the application, one takes approximately an hour to finish. Start the process by using the following link https://www.rev.com/freelancers. Then enter your name and email address to begin your submission. During the application, Rev.com will assess your typing skills, grammar and give you a transcription test which you are required to do and the best part it as no time limit.

The transcription test will have several speakers and what rev wants to test you is whether you can identify the speakers as well as hear what they are saying. After you are done with the test, Click on the submit icon, and an email will be sent to you tanking you for doing the test. A second email will be sent in one or two days if your submission was successful and in case you fail, you will still receive an email, and you can retake the test after 45 days.

Finally, you can now click on the big red icon that says’ complete registration.’ After hitting it, you will be redirected to Rev’s work platform where you sign the user agreement, read a few regulations and you are good to go.

Rev.com Payments

The amount of cash you earn will depend on the audio file difficulty, its length, number of speakers as well their accents. Short files with single speakers often pay less as compared to longer audios with multiple speakers. Payments are made weekly on Mondays where you get paid for all work done from Sunday to Saturday without fail.

Rev Transcription Work Tips

Firstly, the website allows one to listen to an audio file before deciding to work on it. Additionally, one can claim the file, listen to it and if you find it challenging, you can un-claim it within an hour without any penalty so that it can go back on queue allowing another transcribe the opportunity to choose it.

Rev Rules and Regulations

Rev.com requires all applicants to be eighteen years and above, and each person can have just one account. Moreover, once employed by rev.com, the transcriber must never share their details with the clients’ failure to which one gets banned for good. Lastly, you need to follow the Rev’s style guide to the letter as rev.com complaints are given much importance by the admins and could resort to account suspension or termination.

Rev.com Employment Levels

Rev.com jobs are offered at three primary levels Rookie, Revver, and Revver plus with diverse tasks as well as pay. The Rookie-level is for newbies who have just passed their application tests. At this level, the transcriber can only work on short audio files ranging from a minute to 30 minutes. After each submission of a task, they get detailed feedback. After doing tasks totalling to 120 minutes, and have reached specified metrics including accuracy 4.5, formatting 4.5, commitment ratio 8 and on-time submission 75%, the rookie is promoted to Revver.
