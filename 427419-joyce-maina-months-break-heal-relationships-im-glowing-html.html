TV show host Joyce Maina has revealed that she took eight months off relationships to heal from her past relationships.

Joyce Maina on past relationships

The actress narrated that she is currently happy and glowing based on her time to love herself and heal from the consequences that she suffered in her past relationships.

While talking on their TV show on Switch TV about the duration people need to take to heal from broken relationships, the celebrity said:

"Personally as someone who has jumped from relationship to relationship, from the age of 26 to 30, you need to take your time. These past seven to eight months, before I started entertaining conversations, I have been the happiest I have ever been. Everyone around me has noticed, they are like you are glowing and you are so happy. It's because I took time to heal from those relationships," she said.

Maina explained that she was moving back to back in relationships, trying to replace her first fling feeling with the next one.

"Some of the guys in those relationships...you would judge me, let me not even tell you," she further said.
