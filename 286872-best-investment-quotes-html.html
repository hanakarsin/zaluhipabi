Never waste a good crisis because therein lies the best opportunities - Benjamin Franklin

Times of crisis, especially financial and political crises are the best times to invest. Stocks and companies go down, and this is the best time to buy and then sell once the crisis is over. Sadly, most people hoard their money and fail to take advantage of the crisis.

Successful investing is about managing risk, not avoiding it. - Benjamin Franklin

Investments and risk go hand in hand. The strategy is not to invest with the mindset that you will prevent risk but instead look for ways to hedge against risk. Staying informed and up to date with the changes in the market is one way to ensure that you are not caught off guard.
