Monaco Eye Super Eagles Midfielder, Alex Iwobi

April 2024 · 2 minute read

Super Eagles Alex Iwobi is being monitored by French club Monaco, according to reports.

The Nigerian international could be acquired by the former Ligue 1 champions next summer.

The Super Eagles midfielder is wanted by the French club, according to Turkish transfer expert Ekrem Konur.

This season, the versatile midfielder has performed admirably for Everton and has emerged as one of the team’s most important players.

Iwobi has played 14 league games for Frank Lampard’s team, scoring one goal and dishing out six assists.

Additionally, Frank Lampard’s side is attempting to extend his contract after displaying an impressive performance so far this season.

The Nigerian midfielder’s Everton contract will expire at the conclusion of the 2024 summer season.

He joined Everton in 2019 after leaving Arsenal.

What did Watford Manager say about Kalu?

Meanwhile, Watford boss, Slaven Bilic, has applauded Samuel Kalu after the winger significantly contributed to the team’s 1-0 victory over Wigan Athletic last weekend.

Bilic, while speaking to Watford Observer “He’s a player that has got more than something,” 

“He wasn’t fit when we arrived, he still isn’t fully fit, but he’s got something special: a change of pace, a change of direction, good on the ball. His first touch and movement are good, he strikes a ball well.

“He’s a very useful player and we are expecting a lot from him.”
