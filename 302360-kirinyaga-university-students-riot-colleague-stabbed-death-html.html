Students from Kirinyaga University on the night of Wednesday, April 3, went on the rampage causing havoc along the Kerugoya- Kutus road following the death of one of their own.

The second year student identified as Benson Karanja is reported to have been stabbed to death by a group of gangsters and his phone stolen while on his way to an off-campus hostel.
