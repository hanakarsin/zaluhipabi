HIV Positive Woman Urges Fellow Survivors to Accept Their Status

February 2024 · 3 minute read
  • A beautiful woman is certainly not letting her HIV status block her happiness and is encouraging other young survivors to do the same
  • The young woman was recently diagnosed with HIV, proudly sharing her positive status in the heartfelt post
  • Mzansi social media users had a few mixed reactions to the post but it seems most were definitely inspired by her words

A stunning woman has shared her inspirational tale of overcoming hardship, encouraging other HIV+ youth not to let their status get in the way of happiness.

Known as @DingileZikhona_ on social media, the woman took to her Twitter account to share the touching message with her followers.

In spite of the sickness, the young woman stated that she chooses to remain strong for herself and other survivors, and encourages others to accept themselves.

To her, the results of her test do not dictate how she lives and whether she will be happy.

She urged people riding in the same boat to avoid beating themselves up and try looking at life from a positive lens.

"I am HIV positive, so what? Let us see people who are HIV positive and proud to motivate those who are weak. This journey is ours to embrace because we will continue to be survivors."Youths are getting infected every day but they fear to come out and accept their statuses,'' she captioned the inspiring post.

Karuru chose to use his experiences to educate and spread hope for people living with HIV/AIDS and educate those not infected.

He vowed to educate the masses with the little amount of space available on his property to reach the masses with messages of hope.
