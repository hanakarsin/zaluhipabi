WCB musician Mbosso Khan has shared how he turned down a romantic advance from a Mombasa lady who traveled all the way to Tanzania to profess her undying affection towards the star.

The singer revealed how a lady identified as Munira started stalking him all in a bid to get the chance to express her love for him.

Speaking in an interview with Wasafi Media at his palatial home, Mbosso said the young lady jumped in front of his car and refused to move away while demanding audience with him.

According to the star, he was forced to ask one of his assistants to take the smitten woman's number, promising to communicate to her later.

But he did not reach out and thought the lady will just varnish but he was wrong.

She kept showing up in various places including at Diamond's house and asking for Mbosso on end.

''She started becoming a nuisance, calling with different numbers. I ordered my team to block all her numbers. The last time I saw her was at Diamond's gate, I was mad and I asked her to go back home,'' the singer revealed.

Mbosso added that he could not date the lady or help her in any way because she was a stranger to him and he did not want to invite trouble in his court.

''She said she loves me, it is okay, but things do not work that way. I cannot date her, you know I have a family, I have children. It will be stupid of me to just jump into a relationship with a random girl,'' he added.

Mbosso wrapped up by advising the lady to travel back to Kenya and maybe, he may marry her in future after following the right procedure.

''I can advice her to go back home, stay with her people, she never knows I might marry her in future. She clearly knows I can marry up to four wives,'' he said.

His remarks came just hours after Munira came out in public to claim she will end her life should Mbosso turn down her request to marry her.

In an interview with Wasafi Media, the Kenyan said she traveled all the way from Mombasa to Tanzania to get married by the star.
